Welcome to LITS

Empowering Careers via Advanced Technical Expertise

Lumbini Institute of Technical Sciences (LITS) is a leading, CTEVT-affiliated educational center centrally located in Butwal. We deliver specialized diplomas in Health Sciences and technical studies.

Our graduates are ready to enter clinical services or engineering practices, fully equipped with practical competencies. We serve a vast range of student populations across Rupandehi, Palpa, Gulmi, Kapilvastu, and neighboring zones.

Programs We Offer

CTEVT
Duration: 3 Years

Diploma in Pharmacy

Professional health service curriculum covering pharmacology, drug safety, therapeutics, hospital dispensaries, and community pharmacy practice.

CTEVT
Duration: 3 Years

Lab Technician (CMLT)

Thorough clinical laboratory science preparation focused on pathology, clinical microbiology, biochemistry testing protocols, and modern diagnostics.

CTEVT
Duration: 3 Years

Health Assistant (HA)

Rigorous community medical training equipping professional medical assistants for community clinical roles, preventative healthcare, and diagnosis.

CTEVT
Duration: 3 Years

PCL in Radiography

Technical radiography training covering radiation safety protocols, medical imaging procedures, patient care, and diagnostic analysis.

CTEVT
Duration: 3 Years

PCL in Physiotherapy

Hands-on training in rehabilitation science, body kinetics, physical therapy interventions, manual treatments, and long-term diagnostic recovery.
